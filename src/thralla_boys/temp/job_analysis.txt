**Job Description for Machine Learning Product Manager at Dynamo AI:**

**Title**: Machine Learning Product Manager  
**Location**: San Francisco, CA / New York, NY (Remote)  
**Type**: Salaried, Full-Time  

**Responsibilities**:
- Product ownership, covering the full product lifecycle—from defining product vision to detailing product requirements and overseeing product development and launch.
- Develop a deep understanding of customer pain-points, strategic opportunities, and technologies to inform product requirements in the Machine Learning domain.
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams (research, engineering, design, and policy) to introduce groundbreaking AI solutions and bridge the gap between research and real-world production.
- Evaluate product performance and customer feedback to iterate on features and maintain product leadership in the AI landscape.
  
**Qualifications**:
- Experience in building research-driven ML solutions; experience with Computer Vision (CV) or Large Language Models (LLMs) is a plus.
- Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Data Science, Engineering, Mathematics, Physics, Applied Sciences, or a related field.
- Proven ability to create clarity and focus in fast-paced, dynamic environments.
- Strong technical acumen and a willingness to engage with technology intricacies.
- Enjoy building 0-1 products from the ground up.
